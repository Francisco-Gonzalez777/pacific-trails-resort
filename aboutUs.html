A Little About Us

About Us

Nestled between the Pacific ocean and the redwood forest of Big Sur State Park, Pacific Trails Resort has long been a haven for Families, Couples, Hikers, and all seekers of natural beauty. Think “homey” and “rustic” when you think of Pacific Trails Resort, Think of warm, cozy, updated rooms with comfortable beds. Think of close-up, breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and the Santa Lucia Mountains. With the rugged central California Pacific coastline to the west and the dramatic Santa Lucia mountain range to the east, Pacific Trails Resort offers families and adventurers a rejuvenating escape from the hectic pace of everyday life. Enjoy a peaceful, serene vacation in our luxury Yurts nestled in the forest. Amid this enchanting ocean-side setting drawing travelers from around the world for over 60 years to experience the warm hospitality of our Resort. With serene beauty and 35 luxuriously Yurts and suites, Pacific Trails Resort has exciting new memories to create and a view you will never forget.

Our Mission Statement

Pacific Trails Resort is committed to providing our guests with a unique and memorable experience. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where guests can relax, unwind, and connect with nature. Our goal is to provide exceptional service and amenities that exceed our guests' expectations, while preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. We are dedicated to sustainability and responsible tourism, ensuring that future generations can enjoy the beauty of Big Sur for years to come. We believe in creating lasting memories for our guests, and we are passionate about sharing the beauty of our resort with the world. Whether you are seeking adventure or relaxation, Pacific Trails Resort is the perfect destination for your next getaway.
