A Little About Us

About Us

Our luxury Yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each Yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, wooden floors and roof dome that can be opened.

Our Mission Statement

Each Yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities in the lodge.
